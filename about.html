wtf is "baked.cloud" about*

The platform the individual chooses to use, provides the tools (front-end) needed to have meaningful interactions with "the algorithm"(back-end). The individual not the platform decides what a meaningful interaction.

this "baked.cloud" space is going to be designed around this idea of the metaverse...it's "the platform"

"the tools" for now are the playlist and sounds files from the echo chamber (which you can download and use)

"the algorithm" will be the data sonification scripts (more on this soon)
