who is bushsk8r

TLDR:

Beyonce has Sasha Fierce, I have bushsk8r...also chaos theory

  • bush - a wilderness where survival is based on an individuals will to stay live...a d.i.y space where help is not coming...figure it out with the things found
  • sk8r - a rebellious energy rooted in youthful angst. synonymous with punk

Long Version:

when I was younger one of my teachers used to make fun of anyone who walked into class with hair that looked uncombed...saying we looked like bushmen in reference to the San People who are indigenous people from Southern Africa...I am Kenyan and don't have any ancestral ties to the San but I liked the idea of being linked to a pre-colonial past through something as simple as not combing my hair...ever since then every chance that I get to keep my hair uncombed I do.

also when I was younger I was obsessed with watching skate videos...there was a punk rebellious energy that angsty teenage me resonated with... later fell into the youtube rabbit hole of longboaring documentaries and found the flowy smoothness of a longboard is more my speed...also kept hurting my knees trying to kickflip...I don't think I will every find a feeling as freeing as cruising on a longboard

as an extremely introverted black man....a lot of my thought remain in my head...over the years I have found that this is not a health way to live...I needed an outlet that felt safe...bushsk8r is that...it's an alter ego I use to explore and put out ideas which my anxiety normally stops....it's a way I can put my fears and ego aside and channel a flow state letting the purest version of myself create

we all need a way to color outside the lines then seeing what magic happens after taking a step back...does not feel right keeping that child like wonder inside
