"music is my source code" - Virgil Abloh

this all sounds abstract and probably does not make much of sense but this is the "idea in mind" part and hope that it will make more sense once it gets to the "actual thing" part. to do that i am going to be using supercollider to make scripts which can read recipe text files and turn them into audio files

in the mean time we will be vibing out to playlist which i hope you can use to chill out and bake with...it's like a book club but with playlists updated daily... reset at the beginning on the month
