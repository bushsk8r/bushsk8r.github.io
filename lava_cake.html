Baked Chronicles: Volume 0, Issue 3 (cake)

lava cake

the cake is lava

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 oz chocolate
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup flour

process

  1. melt butter and chocolate
  2. add sugar
  3. add egg and yolk
  4. add vanilla
  5. fold in flour into [main mix](do NOT over mix)
  6. split into 2 ramekins
  7. bake @425F for 12 mins
  8. cool in pan for 1 mins
  9. invert and serve
