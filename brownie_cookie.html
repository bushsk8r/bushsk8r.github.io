Baked Chronicles: Volume 0 (bonus recipe)

brownie / hot chocolate cookies

a warm holiday cookie

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup hot chocolate powder
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter (melted)
  • 1 egg
  • 3 TBsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Process

  1. mix flour, baking soda, salt, hot chocolate powder [mix 1]
  2. mix sugars, butter, egg, vanilla, olive oil [mix 2]
  3. add mix 2 into mix 1[main mix](do not over mix)
  4. scoop 1 TBsp per cookie on to baking sheet with parchment paper
  5. bake @ 350 for 9/10 mins
  6. cool on baking sheet for 10 mins
  7. cool on rack for 10 mins
