Baked Chronicles: Volume 0, Issue 3 (cake)

banana rum cake

the cake is rummy

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp cloves
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup butter(room temp)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup rum
  • 3 bananas(mashed)
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 egg(room temp)
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup pecans

process

  1. mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg [dry mix]
  2. mix butter, sugar till fluffy [wet mix/main mix]
  3. add egg, vanilla, sour cream [wet mix/main mix]
  4. add banana, rum [wet mix], may be curdled because butter and egg not at room temp
  5. fold in [dry mix] into [main mix](do NOT over mix)
  6. fold in peacans
  7. grease abundt pan
  8. bake @350F for 45 mins
  9. cool in pan for 15 mins
  10. invert and serve
