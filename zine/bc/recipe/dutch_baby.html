Baked Chronicles: Volume 1 (side quest recipe)

dutch baby

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 3 TBsp sugar
  • few drops of lemon extract
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 3 TBsp butter (split in half)

process

  1. pre heat over with cake pan @ 425F
  2. melt first half of butter (in microwave)
  3. mix flour, sugar, lemon salt
  4. add egg, milk vanilla, melted butter
  5. mix fully (with food processor or blender)[main mix]
  6. melt secong half of butter on hot cake pan (from oven)
  7. add frozen fruit to pan
  8. add main mix to pan
  9. bake for 20 mins @ 425F (DO NOT open oven door)
  10. remove from pan
