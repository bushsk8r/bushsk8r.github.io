Baked Chronicles: Volume 1, Issue 1 (pie crust)

pie crust dough

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter (chilled)
  • 1/3 cup water (cold)

process

  1. mix flour, salt [dry mix]
  2. blend butter into dry mix [main mix]
  3. add water [main mix]
  4. turn on floured surface [dough]
  5. wrap tightly and store in fridge for min:1 hr / max:3 days
  6. roll out to use
